FAQ

Q: How long is each puppy playdate?

A: One Hour!



Q: Can I reserve a playdate with more than one dog?

A: Unfortunately, we cannot accommodate that request.



Q: Where does the playday take place?

A: At your specified location -- possible places include a house, office, or park.



Q: How do I adopt my favorite puppy?

A: We are only a matchmaking service -- to adopt your furry friend, you will have to visit the shelter that the dog resides in.



Q: How many people can play with a dog at one time?

A: It depends -- there’s no maximum, but please be considerate of the dog’s social preferences that are listed on their profile.
